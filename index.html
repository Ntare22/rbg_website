About

Rwa Business Group is a multi-sector business with interests in commodities trading, energy, real estate, ICT and financial services. Through various investments, Rwa Business Group, imports sugar, exports coffee and offers non-banking financial services.
Rwa Business Group was founded in 2012 and is headquartered in Kigali, Rwanda
We strive to be one of Africa's leading and most dynamic and vibrant diversified conglomerates, with the main pursuit being wealth creation for not just ourselves but the average person in the country we do business.
